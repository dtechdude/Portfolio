Hello, I'm David Adedeji, a product designer and user interface engineer, highly-experienced in balancing complex business goals with user-centered solutions.

I specialize in design systems, design thinking, HTML, CSS, SCSS, and Webflow. I also speak French fluently.

I'm a product designer based out of Lagos Nigeria. I started my career as a graphic designer after attending a design school called Aptech institute where I learnt the principles of user experience design, color theory, layout, typography, accessibility, and other design principles. This built the foundation for what was to become my passion - product design.

Fast forward to the present day I now have real-world experience in product design, and front-end static coding. I have created websites and applications for a wide range of companies from the Energy to Apparel to Arts and Entertainment industries.

Aside from all the work stuff, I love playing video games and watching movies. Recently I've developed a profound love for the web 3 space.
